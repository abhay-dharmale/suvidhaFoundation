Suvidha Foundation (Suvidha Mahila Mandal)

About

Suvidha Mahila Mandal is a non-profit organization working to impart education among the financially challenged sections to help them realize parity in education and strength of little minds in building a promising future. The organization has provisions of student internships, student mentorship and the scope to volunteer. Through these programmes, the organization aims to achieve the vision of imparting innovative education that stays with the students forever and equip them for the unforeseen future.

Mission

"To inspire students, empower them to innovate, and facilitate their integration into diverse fields to collectively shape the trajectory of the next generation of humankind, fostering a global community of forward-thinkers and change-makers. Additionally, to spearhead impactful initiatives, such as food donation drives and awareness campaigns, specifically targeted at rural regions, addressing critical issues of hunger and food insecurity, while simultaneously raising consciousness about the importance of social responsibility and collective action in fostering sustainable development and equitable opportunities for all."

Vision

"To cultivate the next generation of entrepreneurs, our vision entails offering skill-based education, empowering individuals with the knowledge, tools, and mindset necessary to thrive in today's dynamic business landscape, fostering innovation, resilience, and ethical leadership. Furthermore, we aspire to extend our reach globally, by providing internship opportunities, training programs, and workshops designed to equip individuals with practical skills and real-world experience. Our commitment to quality education knows no bounds, as we aim to transcend geographical barriers and ensure access to high-quality educational resources and opportunities for personal and professional growth for individuals worldwide."
