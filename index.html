Suvidha Foundation (Suvidha Mahila Mandal)

About

Suvidha Mahila Mandal is a non-profit organization working to impart education among the financially challenged sections to help them realize parity in education and strength of little minds in building a promising future. The organization has provisions of student internships, student mentorship and the scope to volunteer.

Mission

"To inspire students, empower them to innovate, and facilitate their integration into diverse fields to collectively shape the trajectory of the next generation of humankind, fostering a global community of forward-thinkers and change-makers. Additionally, to spearhead impactful initiatives, such as food donation drives and awareness campaigns, specifically targeted at rural regions"

Vision

"To cultivate the next generation of entrepreneurs, our vision entails offering skill-based education, empowering individuals with the knowledge, tools, and mindset necessary to thrive in today's dynamic business landscape, fostering innovation, resilience, and ethical leadership. Furthermore, we aspire to extend our reach globally"

Apply For Internship and Mentorship Programs

Seize the opportunity to gain invaluable experience and jumpstart your career with an internship that propels you towards success.

The Suvidha Foundation Internship Program is a unique opportunity for students and recent graduates to gain experience and contribute to the work of Suvidha Foundation. Along with undertaking tasks related to their specific skills, we encourage interns to develop a reputable professional portfolio.
